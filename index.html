About Us

Welcome to Eze Green Enterprises! We provide eco-friendly alternatives to single-use plastic. Support us to make this planet greener and safer. We mainly deal in eco-friendly plastic free shopping bags, garbage bags etc. At Eze Green Enterprises, we believe in a cleaner, greener future for everyone. Established with a vision to reduce plastic pollution, we specialize in producing high-quality, bio-degradable carry bags and eco-friendly packaging solutions. Our commitment to sustainability drives us to provide alternatives that are both practical and environmentally responsible. Registered under ID: HP10439550, our manufacturing unit is located in the beautiful state of Himachal Pradesh. We focus on creating durable, plastic-free shopping bags, garbage bags, and other packaging products designed to replace harmful single-use plastics. Each product is crafted to help individuals and businesses make the shift towards a sustainable lifestyle without compromising on quality or convenience. At the heart of our work is a mission to support national initiatives like the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and Make in India by offering products proudly manufactured in India.
